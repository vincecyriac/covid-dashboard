FAQs

What are your sources?

I am collecting this data from the api: api.covid19india.org & diseash.sh .
api.covid19india.org is using state bulletins and official handles to update our numbers. The data is validated by a group of volunteers and published into a Google sheet and an API.
disease.sh update the data from MOHFW website.

Why does your website have difference in numbers compared to MOHFW website?

MoHFW updates the data at a scheduled time. However, we update them based on state press bulletins, official (CM, Health M) handles, PBI, Press Trust of India, ANI reports. These are generally more recent.

why district wise data is note avalable at the initial loading?

Showing the data of all districts in India is not that much efficient! So the district data gets updated based on the state you are selected from the map or deopdown menu.

Is this is the final version of your website?

No! This is the phase one release. I am still working on this. More new features will be added in the upcoming days.
